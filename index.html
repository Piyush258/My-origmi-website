Intresting Crane facts

  • Cranes are powerful flyers
    with some having the ability to fly over the Himalayas.
  • Cranes are omnivorous
    long-legged wading birds that eat a variety of plants and animals
  • During migration cranes fly with their head and neck straight out
    and their feet and legs straight behind them
  • The whooping crane (Grus americana) is the tallest American bird
    with adults standing to 1.5 m (5 ft.) tall

Intresting Parrot Facts

  • Parrots are smart. Parrots learn well, and they learn fast.
  • Not all parrots can fly.
  • Parrots can imitate sounds.
    • They rely on seeds for food.

Intresting Facts About Pandas

  • Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
